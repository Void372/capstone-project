About Us

Welcome to McDonald's

Founded in 1940, McDonald's is the world's leading global foodservice retailer with over 39,000 locations in over 100 countries. We serve more than 69 million customers each day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make delicious feel-good moments easy for everyone. We are committed to providing quality food, excellent service, and a welcoming environment for our customers.

Our History

McDonald's began as a small drive-in restaurant in San Bernardino, California, and has grown into a global brand. Our iconic Golden Arches and signature menu items, such as the Big Mac and Chicken McNuggets, have become synonymous with fast food.

Community Engagement

We believe in giving back to the communities we serve. Through initiatives like the Ronald McDonald House Charities, we support families in need and promote children's health and education.
